PACHYCEPHALOSAURUS
Era: Late Cretaceous (around 70-66 million years ago).
Features: Pachycephalosaurus is famous for its thick, bony dome on top of its head, which could be up to 10 inches (25 cm) thick. The dome was surrounded by small bony knobs and spikes.
PTERANODON
Era: Late Cretaceous
Features: Large pterosaur with wings spanning up to 33 feet.
Size: Wingspan up to 33 feet.
